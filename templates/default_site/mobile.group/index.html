About Chandon Summer

Welcome to Chandon Summer, where the sun always shines, the cocktails create a stir, and there’s a little bit of beach in every bottle.

Since 2011, we’ve been releasing limited-edition summer bottles that capture the spirit of the season. This year’s third-anniversary bottle was inspired by seaside dreams and poolside parties, with a distinctly nautical vibe.

But just like summer, Chandon’s limited-edition summer bottle won’t last forever. Get yours now before they’re gone.
